Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

What we collect

When you request a quote or place an order we collect the details you share with us — name, email, phone, shipping address, and any notes about your media. We also record the media we receive so we can return it to you.

What we do with your media

Your originals stay on-site in our Portland studio and are never sent to third parties. Digital copies are kept encrypted on our servers for 30 days after delivery — so you can request a re-download — and then permanently deleted.

We never sell, rent, publish, or share your digitized content. You retain full ownership of every file we create.

Cookies & analytics

Our website uses a privacy-respecting analytics service that does not set identifying cookies. We do not track visitors across other sites. Essential cookies (session, CSRF) are used only when you are actively placing an order.

Your rights

You can request a copy of any data we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it at any time. Email privacy@vintage2digital.com and we'll respond within 30 days.

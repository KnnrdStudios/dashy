Every shipment is tracked and insured for up to $10,000 per box. While we handle media with extreme care, some items — particularly aged or damaged tapes and reels — may be irreparable. We inspect all items on arrival and notify you before proceeding with items we flag as high-risk.

Standard turnaround is 7–14 business days from receipt of media. Priority turnaround times are available for an additional fee. Turnaround estimates exclude shipping and may be extended for large orders or restoration work.

Orders are paid in full at the time of booking. Additional work (restoration, extra output formats) is quoted and approved by you before work begins.

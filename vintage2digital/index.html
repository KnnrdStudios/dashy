Our story

Built by archivists.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Is my media safe in transit?

Every box ships in a foam-lined, tracked, and insured kit (up to $10,000). On arrival, each item is barcoded and logged in our chain-of-custody system — you'll get a notification at every step.

How long does a typical order take?

Most orders complete in 7–14 business days. Priority turnarounds (48–72 hours) are available for time-sensitive projects — just let us know when you order.

What if my tape is damaged or moldy?

Don't throw it out! Our restoration lab specializes in rescuing damaged media — including moldy, warped, or snapped tapes. We'll assess the condition free of charge and quote repair work before proceeding.

Who owns the digitized files?

You do. Always. We never claim rights, resell, or share your media. After delivery we hold an encrypted backup for 30 days in case you need a re-download, then it's permanently deleted.

Can you digitize international formats (PAL / SECAM)?

Absolutely. We have dedicated PAL and SECAM decks and can deliver NTSC-compatible files or preserve original format — your call.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes — digital gift cards from $50 upward are available year-round. They're the most requested holiday gift for parents and grandparents.
